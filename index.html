3D PRINTED DESIGN

Crafted with precision, Trail is 3D printed using millions of intricate lines that create the perfect blend of support and flexibility. Built for outdoor adventure, from forest paths to mountain trails, this is footwear engineered to take you further.

ADVANCED MATERIALS

Built with ZellerFOAM TPU technology, Trail delivers both comfort and durability. The responsive cushioning adapts to every terrain, while the construction ensures lasting performance through stream crossings, rocky paths, and countless miles of adventure.

CUSTOM FIT

Using Zellerfeld's 3D printing technology, each Trail is custom-fitted to your unique foot shape. This approach ensures optimal comfort and support, whether you're crossing meadows, climbing hills, or exploring new paths.

SUSTAINABILITY

Innovative 3D printing processes creates zero material waste, using only what each shoe requires. Trail's materials are fully recyclable, and on-demand production eliminates excess inventory. Experience performance footwear that's better for the planet.
